descriptionJob Description

The Coffee House is looking for enthusiastic and dedicated baristas to join our morning team at our flagship District 1 location. This is an excellent opportunity for students who want to earn income while learning the art of specialty coffee.

As a barista, you will be responsible for crafting high-quality coffee beverages, engaging with our loyal customers, and maintaining a clean and inviting café environment during the busy morning rush hours.

We foster a supportive team culture where every team member is valued and encouraged to grow. Whether you're a coffee enthusiast or just looking for a great part-time opportunity, we'd love to hear from you!
